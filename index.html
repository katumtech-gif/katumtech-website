About Katumtech Engineers

Founded in Nairobi, Katumtech Engineers is a dedicated printer sales and service company focused on delivering reliable printing solutions to small businesses, corporates, schools and government offices. Our team is built from experienced technicians and procurement specialists who understand printer lifecycles, replacement costs, and the importance of uptime.

Our mission

To provide honest, cost-effective printing solutions that keep offices productive and equipment performing optimally.

Our values

  • Transparency — clear pricing and genuine parts.
  • Speed — fast diagnostics and repairs.
  • Professionalism — trained technicians and documentation.
  • Customer-first — warranty and follow-up support.

Team & capabilities

We handle installations, maintenance contracts, emergency repairs and spare parts procurement. Our technicians are trained on brands including HP, Canon, Epson, Nashuatec and more. We provide both on-site servicing and in-shop repairs.
